# Carnation

### Carnations are known for their ruffled features, vibrant colors, and rich symbolism, making them a favorite in floral arrangementst and gardens

Common colors for Carnations include:
- Pink
- Red
- White
- Yellow
- Purple

These flowers typically grow to be 18-30 inches tall depending on the variety.
Three types of carnations, Large-Flowered Carnations, Dwarf-Flowered Carnations, and Spray Carnations

### Care Tips:
1. Use well-drained soil and give at least 6 hours of direct sunlight daily
2. Water carnations once a week, allowing the soil to dry out completly between waterings to prevent root rot
3. Regularly remove dead/spent flowers to encourage flowers to bloom
