# Daisy

### Daisies are birhgt flowers known for their adaptability. They can symbolize innocence, purity, and new beginnings. 

Common daisy varieties include:
- Oxeye Daisy
- Shasta Daisy
- English Daisy
- Gerbera Daisy

Daisies can live in various environments, sunny or shaded areas, and grow on almost every continent. 

### Care Tips:
1. Plant in full sun for optimal growth, make sure they get at least 6 hours of sunlight a day
2. Water daisies regularly, 1-2 inches per week
3. Use a well-drained, nutrient-rich soil 
4. Fertilize every six weeks with a balanced fetilizer
5. Add a layer of mulch to help retain moisture and moderate soil temperature
