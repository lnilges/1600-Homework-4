# Lily

### Lilies are popular flowers in gardens and floral arangements. They have been associated with purity and innocence in various culture. They also have medicinal properties and are used in traditional remedies. 

Popular specicies of Lilies:
- Asiatic Lilies
- Oriental Lilies
- Trumpet Lilies
- Tiger Lilies

Lilies typically have tall stems with narrow leaves and large flowers that can be trumpet-shaped or bowl-shaped. They can grow between 1 to 6 feet tall, depending on the species. Lilies come in a variety of colors including, white, yellow, orange, pink, and red. 

### Care Tips:
1. Use well-drained, enriched soil that is moderately acidic
2. Lilies thrive in at least 6 hours of full sunlight
3. Consistenly water is needed but avoid overwatering to prevent root rot
4. Look out for pests like aphids that can eat the flowers
5. For taller lilies, use stakes to help support the weight and prevent leaning
